Our Capabilities

Explore our services for expert HVAC, furnace, duct, AC, and basement cleaning, ensuring a clean and comfortable home.

Furnace & Vent Cleaning

Enhance your furnace's efficiency and lifespan with our professional cleaning services, including coil, fan, and burner maintenance. Improve air quality and lower energy costs.

Basement Line Cleaning

Our basement line cleaning targets input and output lines, improving airflow and creating a healthier home. Clean ducts reduce dust, allergens, and mold for optimal HVAC performance.

Hot Water Tank Cleaning

We provide hot water tank cleaning as part of our service package, draining and cleaning the chimney to remove sediment build-up. This service improves efficiency and extends the life of your hot water system.

Humidifier Inspection

As part of our humidifier maintenance services, we offer free inspections to ensure your humidifier is functioning correctly. A well-maintained humidifier can help control indoor humidity, reducing dry air and improving overall comfort in your home.

Furnace Filter Replacement

Our service includes a standard-size furnace filter replacement, ensuring your HVAC system operates efficiently. A clean furnace filter helps trap dust, allergens, and pollutants, contributing to cleaner indoor air.

Ventilation System Sanitization

This service includes applying a sanitization spray that helps eliminate bacteria, mold, and allergens from your entire air duct system, leaving your home’s air cleaner and fresher.

FAQ

What services are included in furnace cleaning?

Our furnace cleaning service includes a thorough cleaning of your entire furnace, covering essential parts such as the coil, fan, grill, motor, burner, and sensitive switches. This ensures your furnace runs efficiently and extends its lifespan.

How often should I get my furnace cleaned?

It is recommended to clean your furnace once a year, especially before the winter season. If it's been more than a year since your last cleaning, now is the perfect time to schedule the service.

What is included in basement line cleaning?

Our basement line cleaning covers the two main lines in your basement: the input and output lines. We professionally clean these lines to improve airflow and ensure your system runs efficiently.

Is hot water tank cleaning included?

Yes! We offer free cleaning of one hot water tank in every package. If you have two hot water tanks, we can clean the second one for a small additional charge of $25.

Do you offer humidifier inspections?

Yes, a free humidifier inspection is included in our package. We check your humidifier to ensure it is working properly and efficiently.

How many vents are included in the vent cleaning service?

Our package includes free cleaning of up to 10 vents. If you have more than 10 vents, there is a charge of $7 per additional vent (normally $9).

What is the cost of furnace cleaning?

1 furnace: $119 2 furnaces: $179 This includes the complete cleaning of your furnace and basic parts, plus basement line cleaning.

Are there any hidden charges?

No, there are no hidden charges. The prices you see in our packages are the only amounts you will pay. We believe in full transparency, so there are no extra or hidden fees.

What is included in the sanitization service?

Our sanitization service includes a free sanitization spray for your entire home’s ventilation system, ensuring that the air circulating in your home is clean and free of bacteria and mold.

How often should I clean my vents?

Vents should be cleaned at least once every 1-2 years to prevent dust, allergens, and other debris from building up. Regular vent cleaning helps improve the quality of air in your home.

Can I get a discount for cleaning more than 10 vents?

Yes! Our current promotion offers $7 per additional vent (normally $9). This allows you to save more when cleaning a larger number of vents.

Do you offer a warranty on your cleaning services?

Yes! With our Premium Plan, you get an extended warranty on all cleaning services. This ensures your peace of mind and guarantees the longevity of our work.

Do you offer same-day or emergency services?

Yes, we offer priority scheduling with our Pro Plan and Premium Plan to help you get faster service. For urgent needs, please contact us directly.

How do I schedule an appointment?

You can schedule an appointment by contacting us directly through our website or by giving us a call. We will find a time that works best for you and confirm your booking.

What if I need to reschedule or cancel?

No problem! You can easily reschedule or cancel your appointment by giving us a call at least 24 hours in advance. We’ll work with you to find another convenient time.
